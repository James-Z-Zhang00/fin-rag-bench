ACME Corporation 10-K Annual Report 2023

Company Overview:
ACME Corporation (ticker: ACME) is a technology company headquartered in San Francisco, California. The company was founded in 2015 by CEO Jane Smith and CFO Robert Chen.

Financial Highlights:
For the fiscal year ending December 31, 2023, ACME Corporation reported:
- Total Revenue: $450 million (up 25% from $360 million in 2022)
- Net Income: $85 million (up 30% from $65 million in 2022)
- Operating Cash Flow: $120 million
- Total Assets: $800 million
- Total Liabilities: $200 million
- Shareholders Equity: $600 million

Business Segments:
ACME operates in three primary segments:
1. Cloud Services (60% of revenue) - $270 million
2. Enterprise Software (30% of revenue) - $135 million  
3. Consulting Services (10% of revenue) - $45 million

Risk Factors:
The company faces competition from Microsoft, Amazon Web Services, and Google Cloud Platform in the cloud services market. Economic recession could impact enterprise spending on software solutions.

Management Team:
- Jane Smith, CEO (Founder, former VP at Oracle)
- Robert Chen, CFO (Former Finance Director at Salesforce)
- David Johnson, CTO (Previously led engineering at Dropbox)

Stock Performance:
ACME stock traded at $85 per share at year-end 2023, giving the company a market capitalization of approximately $8.5 billion. The stock price increased 40% during 2023.
