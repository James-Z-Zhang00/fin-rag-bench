TechCorp Inc. Q4 2023 Earnings Report

Quarter Ended: December 31, 2023

Executive Summary:
TechCorp Inc. (NASDAQ: TECH), a leading artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions provider, announced strong fourth quarter results that exceeded analyst expectations.

Q4 2023 Financial Results:
- Revenue: $180 million (vs. $150 million Q4 2022, +20% YoY)
- Gross Margin: 72% (improved from 68% in Q4 2022)
- Operating Income: $45 million
- Earnings Per Share (EPS): $1.85 (vs. analyst consensus of $1.65)
- Free Cash Flow: $50 million

Full Year 2023 Results:
- Annual Revenue: $650 million (vs. $520 million in 2022, +25% growth)
- Annual Net Income: $160 million
- Research & Development Spending: $130 million (20% of revenue)

Product Performance:
TechCorp's AI platform "NeuralCore" saw 45% revenue growth, now accounting for 55% of total revenue. The company added 850 new enterprise customers in Q4, bringing total customer count to 4,200.

Geographic Revenue Breakdown:
- North America: 60% ($390 million)
- Europe: 25% ($162.5 million)
- Asia Pacific: 15% ($97.5 million)

Strategic Partnerships:
TechCorp announced partnership with IBM for joint AI research and signed a distribution agreement with SAP for enterprise markets in Europe.

2024 Guidance:
Management projects 2024 revenue between $780-820 million, representing 20-26% growth. Expected EPS range: $7.50-$8.00.

CEO Statement:
CEO Maria Rodriguez stated: "Our AI platform continues to gain market share. The partnership with IBM strengthens our position in enterprise AI solutions."

CFO Commentary:
CFO Thomas Williams noted: "Strong cash generation enables increased R&D investment while maintaining healthy margins."
